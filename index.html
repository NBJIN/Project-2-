Memory Game Catch The Black Cat

Instructions:

Player can choose two levels. Level Easy is where the player gets 30 seconds and a speed of 1000 for the cat to display. Level Hard is where the player gets 20 seconds and a speed of 700 for the cat to display. Once the player has chosen their desired level the time and score counter will reflect the chosen level. The player then click on Start Game to begin playing the game. Once the game starts the timer will count down and any successful trys will be recorded in the score counter. When the game finishes an alert will display advising the player the game has finished and will also display the score counter.
