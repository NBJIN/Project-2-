× Instructions For Catch The Black Cat Game
The player can choose to play at two levels. When the page loads the easy level will be preselected but the player can easily choose the hard level by clicking on the hard button.
Level "Easy" is where the player gets 30 seconds and the cat will display at a speed of 1000 milliseconds.
Level "Hard" is where the player gets 20 seconds and the cat will display at a speed of 700 milliseconds.
Once the player has chosen their desired level, the timer will reflect the chosen level. The player then clicks on Start Game to begin playing the game. Once the game starts the timer will count down and any successful trys will be recorded in the score counter. When the game finishes an alert will display advising the player the game has finished and will also display the final score. If the player would like to play again at the same level they just click on start game again and the game will start counting down. If the player wants to reset the game they can click Reset Game button and this will reset the time remaining to 0 and score counter to 0. Then they can choose to play at the level selected or at the alternative level.
